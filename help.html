A Simple Guide for the Website

Here is a simple guide on using the eSakayMo website!


1. Creating a new account

It is highly encouraged to register an account here since it saves important data regarding fare transportation which makes it easier to access. On the top right corner, there should be a button that says 'Create Account' which will take you to a registration form. After filling everything, you're all set!


2. Deciding on a location

MRT fares depend on how far your location is. Here, there are several options you can choose for the pick-up and drop-off location. Each of them have the regular fare and the discounted fare which is displayed on the right side of the screen. In that way, it can help you be aware of the cost.
